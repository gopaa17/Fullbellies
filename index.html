How It Works

A simple loop that feeds people, reduces waste, and keeps businesses thriving.

📦

Surplus Listed

Restaurants, hotels & cloud kitchens list their surplus food in real time—before it goes to waste.

🚀

Matched & Delivered

Our platform matches meals to nearby people, NGOs, or community fridges and coordinates fast delivery.

Plates Filled

Mission-Driven, Profit-Smart

Seven revenue streams that keep us sustainable while we feed the world.

Commissions

Small fee on each discounted meal transaction from food providers.

Subscriptions

Premium plans for restaurants with analytics, badges, and priority placement.

Logistics Fees

Delivery and pickup fees built into the platform's fulfilment layer.

CSR Partnerships

Corporates sponsor meals in bulk—great impact reports for their CSR goals.

Pay It Forward

Users donate a meal for someone else—micro-donations that add up fast.

NGO Tools

SaaS dashboard for NGOs to manage food distribution, volunteers, and reporting.
